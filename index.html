Tips for a Successful Wedding Day Timeline

Creating an effective wedding day timeline is crucial for ensuring everything runs smoothly. Here are five essential tips from Annie:

  1. Plan Ahead: Create a detailed timeline well before the wedding day to avoid last-minute stress.
  2. Allocate Time for Photos: Ensure there's enough time set aside for both posed and candid shots throughout the day.
  3. Communicate with Vendors: Keep all vendors informed about the timeline so everyone is on the same page.
  4. Prioritize Key Moments: Identify must-have shots and communicate these to your photographer ahead of time.
  5. Stay Flexible: While having a plan is important, be open to spontaneous moments that may arise during the day.
